( The Process )

I was using a website and was captivated by the amazing animations because they made me actually want to perform and action on this website. Then and there, I decided to go on a survey to see if people felt the same .

( The Types of Users )

I discovered that they were three types of users one was like me and appreciated the animations . The second was simply annoyed by them and the third couldnt even care less .

( Final Verdict )

All in all, what users want is a beautiful and fully functional website . A website that gets them there with the shortest possible time . " Parks ®️ " is a concept that attempts to satisfy the three parties . It doesn't have to be a tug of war .
