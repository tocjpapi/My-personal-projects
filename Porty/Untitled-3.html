About

Hey! I’m Treasure, a 16-year-old web designer. I believe in the

beauty of simplicity, where less is truly more. My passion lies in creating

elegant and designs that resonate with clarity and purpose.

Whether it’s a clean user interface, a serene piece of writing, or a

thoughtfully composed photograph, I find joy in stripping away the

unnecessary to reveal the essence of what truly matters. Join me on this

journey of simplicity, where every pixel, word, and breath counts.

Explore my work by clicking on the images on the portfolio section.

Let’s create something remarkable together.

The Journey

In the bustling streets of Lagos, I emerged from humble

beginnings with dreams that seemed to have stretched beyond the

horizon. Pan-Atlantic University in Lekki became my refuge, where

mentors fueled my passions and exams tested my resilience.

Through God helping me in studies and lots of good friendships, facilitating my

connections made, I clung to the belief that education was my compass.

Stripping away distractions, I found clarity, and in code and

canvas, I expressed the elegance of simplicity. Pan-Atlantic University,

rooted in St. Josemaria Escrivá's teachings, became the canvas where my

dreams are starting to evolve into tangible realities.
